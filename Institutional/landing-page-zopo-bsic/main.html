What is the Z⬡P⬡ Ecosystem?

Z⬡P⬡ is a decentralized application (DApp) developed on the Ethereum blockchain that allows Citizens and Companies to get involved in cleaning PLASTIC waste in seas, streets, rivers and other tangible surfaces.

How does it work?

Initially, you need to access the DApp using your MetaMask account. New users can choose to register as Citizens or Checkpoints in the MVP version. Citizens should search for the nearest Checkpoints using the "Find Checkpoint" feature. Upon reaching the Checkpoints, Citizens will be able to collect appropriate bags to collect plastic waste. After collecting, the Citizen must return to a Checkpoint to start the weighing process.

Benefits

The most important benefit that Ecosystem Personas can have is the satisfaction in helping the nature recovery. But, as a reward, a payment will be made in ERC20 Tokens proportionally to the amount of garbage collected. These Tokens will be Minted directly in the Wallets of those who participated in the process! In the future, these Tokens will have well-defined uses, such as discounts at accredited establishments, discounts on Government taxes, among other possibilities.

What we most value and propose:

Engaging Citizens

Z⬡P⬡'s main objective is to encourage Citizens and Entrepreneurs from around the world on this journey in favor of the environment.

Earth Friendly

All stages of the Ecosystem were designed in such a way that EVERYTHING can be reused - never wasted.

Trustfull Tracking

By using Ethereum SmartContracts and Analytics, we can have the complete end-to-end tracking of the collected and produced Plastic.

Valuing People

Valuation is the key to the well functioning of the Ecosystem. Every participant role will be rewarded proportionally trhough ERC20 Tokens.
